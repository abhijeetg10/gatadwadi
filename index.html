About Gatadwadi

Gatadwadi is a small yet distinct village nestled in the Walwa tehsil of Sangli district, within the Indian state of Maharashtra. Known for its charm, it contributes to the cultural and demographic richness of the region.

Taluka Walwa (वाळवा)
District Sangli (सांगली)
State Maharashtra (महाराष्ट्र)
Total Population 1,586+
